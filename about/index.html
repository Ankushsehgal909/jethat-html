About Us - FAQ

Learn more about JetHat Cyber Security and our journey

JetHat Cyber Security was founded in 2018 with a vision to protect businesses from evolving cyber threats through innovative solutions. Since our inception, we have been at the forefront of digital protection, combining cutting-edge technology with unwavering commitment to security excellence.

Our mission is to empower businesses with secure, scalable digital transformation solutions that protect against cyber threats while enabling innovation and growth. We provide comprehensive cybersecurity protection, cutting-edge AI and ML solutions, and 24/7 support and monitoring.

Our core values are Security First (security is at the core of everything we do), Innovation (constantly pushing technological boundaries), Collaboration (working together for mutual success), and Integrity (honest and ethical in all our dealings).

Since our foundation in 2018, we expanded our services in 2020 to include AI/ML development and cloud solutions, serving clients across multiple industries. By 2023, we launched groundbreaking AI products and established ourselves as leaders in cybersecurity and digital transformation.

Our 2024-2025 roadmap includes launching the Quantum Security Suite (Q2 2024), Neural Network Optimizer (Q3 2024), Metaverse Security Suite (Q4 2024), and Quantum ML Platform (Q1 2025). These innovations will further strengthen our position as industry leaders.

JetHat stands out through our unique combination of cybersecurity expertise and AI/ML innovation. We don't just protect against current threats - we anticipate future challenges and develop cutting-edge solutions. Our commitment to security-first innovation and collaborative partnerships sets us apart in the industry.
