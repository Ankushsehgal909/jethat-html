Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation and refund policy for various services and subscriptions.

Last Updated: January 10, 2025

1. Service Cancellation

Clients may cancel services subject to the following conditions:

  • Written notice must be provided as per the service agreement
  • Cancellation terms vary by service type and contract duration
  • Ongoing projects may require completion of current milestones
  • Data migration and handover procedures must be completed

2. Consulting Services

For consulting and professional services:

  • 24-hour notice: Full refund for unused sessions
  • Same-day cancellation: 50% charge may apply
  • No-show: Full session charge applies
  • Project-based: Charges apply for completed work

3. Subscription Services

For recurring subscription services:

  • Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled with 30 days notice
  • Annual subscriptions require 60 days notice
  • Refunds are prorated based on unused service period
  • Enterprise contracts follow individual agreement terms

4. Development Projects

For custom development work:

  • Cancellation allowed up to 48 hours after project initiation
  • Work completed up to cancellation point will be charged
  • Source code and deliverables provided for paid work
  • Milestone-based projects charged per completed milestone

5. Emergency Services

For emergency cybersecurity response:

  • Cancellation not applicable once response is initiated
  • Minimum charges apply for emergency callouts
  • Full charges apply if cancellation occurs after technician dispatch

6. Refund Processing

Refund processing timeline:

  • Refund requests processed within 5-7 business days
  • Bank transfers may take 3-5 additional business days
  • International transfers may take up to 10 business days
  • Processing fees may be deducted from refund amount

7. Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure events (natural disasters, pandemics, etc.), special cancellation terms may apply. We will work with clients to find mutually acceptable solutions.

8. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with cancellation charges or refund amounts, please contact our customer service team. We aim to resolve all disputes amicably and fairly.
